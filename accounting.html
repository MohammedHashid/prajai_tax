Frequently Asked Questions:

1. What is bookkeeping?

Bookkeeping involves recording daily financial transactions such as sales, purchases, receipts, and payments in an organized manner.

2. Why is accounting important for my business?

Proper accounting helps you track income and expenses, manage cash flow, comply with regulations, and make informed business decisions.

3. How often should I update my books?

It is recommended to update your books regularly—daily or weekly—to ensure accurate financial records and avoid last-minute issues.

4. Can you prepare financial reports?

Yes, we prepare financial reports such as profit & loss statements, balance sheets, and cash flow reports for better business insights.

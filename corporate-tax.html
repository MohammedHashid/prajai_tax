Frequently Asked Questions:

1. What is corporate tax?

Corporate tax is the tax imposed on the income or profits of companies and corporations.

2. Who is required to pay corporate tax?

All registered companies and corporate entities earning income are required to pay corporate tax as per applicable laws.

3. What services are included in corporate tax?

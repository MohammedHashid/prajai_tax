Frequently Asked Questions:

1. Who needs to register for GST?

Businesses with turnover exceeding the prescribed limit or those engaged in interstate supply must register for GST. Certain businesses are required to register regardless of turnover.

2. What documents are required for GST registration?

Documents include PAN card, Aadhaar card, business registration proof, address proof, bank details, and photographs of the owner or partners.

3. How often do I need to file GST returns?

GST returns are usually filed monthly, quarterly, or annually depending on your business type and turnover.

4. What happens if I don’t file GST returns on time?

Late filing may result in penalties, interest, and suspension of GST registration. Timely filing is essential to avoid issues.

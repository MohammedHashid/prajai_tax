Frequently Asked Questions:

1. What is business valuation?

Business valuation is the process of determining the economic value of a business based on its financial performance, assets, and market conditions.

2. Why is business valuation important?

It helps in making informed decisions for mergers, acquisitions, investments, and business planning.

3. When do I need a business valuation?

You may need valuation during fundraising, selling a business, mergers, legal disputes, or strategic planning.

4. What factors affect business valuation?

Factors include revenue, profitability, assets, liabilities, market trends, and growth potential.

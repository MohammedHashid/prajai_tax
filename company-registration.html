Frequently Asked Questions:

1. What types of companies can be registered?

You can register Private Limited Companies, LLPs, Partnership firms, and sole proprietorships based on your business needs.

2. What documents are required for company registration?

Common documents include PAN card, Aadhaar card, address proof, photographs, and business address details of directors or partners.

3. How long does it take to register a company?

Company registration usually takes 7–10 working days, depending on document verification and approval from authorities.

4. Do I need GST registration along with company registration?

GST registration is required based on your business type and turnover. We can assist you with both company and GST registration.

Frequently Asked Questions:

1. Who needs to file Income Tax returns?

Any individual or business earning income above the basic exemption limit must file Income Tax returns. Filing is also required for claiming refunds, applying for loans, or maintaining financial records.

2. What documents are required for filing Income Tax?

Common documents include PAN card, Aadhaar card, Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs, salary slips, and details of any additional income such as rent or business earnings.

3. What happens if I file my Income Tax return late?

Late filing may attract penalties, interest on unpaid taxes, and loss of certain benefits like carrying forward losses. It is always recommended to file before the due date.

4. Can I claim deductions to reduce my tax?

Yes, you can claim deductions under various sections such as 80C, 80D, and 80G. These include investments, insurance premiums, and eligible expenses that help reduce your taxable income.

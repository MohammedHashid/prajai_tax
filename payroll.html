Frequently Asked Questions:

1. What is payroll management?

Payroll management involves processing employee salaries, deductions, bonuses, and maintaining records while ensuring compliance with statutory requirements.

2. What are the components of payroll?

Payroll includes salary, allowances, bonuses, deductions such as PF, ESI, TDS, and net pay calculation.

3. Why is payroll important for businesses?

Payroll ensures employees are paid accurately and on time while maintaining compliance with legal requirements and tax regulations.

4. What happens if payroll compliance is not maintained?

Non-compliance may lead to penalties, legal issues, and employee dissatisfaction. Proper payroll management avoids such risks.
